A little about the artist and creator

Padma Shri Awardee Jivya Soma Mashe is one of the most widely recognised names in the world of Warli style painting. He transformed a centuries-old cultural language into an artistic practice. His artwork is proudly chosen by art lovers, collectors and investors across the world. Read more about Jivya Soma Mashe here

Nadeem Riyasat Ali Shaikh was born without hands in Mumbai, on the 10th of November 1996. From a very young age, Nadeem had to carry out every day to day the activities with his feet. At school, he began to paint and draw with his feet and also successfully completed his Board Exams by writing with his foot.

Nadeem has had the opportunity to participate in several painting competitions and exhibitions, as a member of MFPA. He is free-spirited and very passionate about his art. He loves sketching portraits of personalities that inspire him. He’s been a member of MFPA since 2008

Seema Kohli is an Indian contemporary artist, sculptor and poet. She has worked across painting, sculpture and installation.

Seema Kohli Born in 1960, Seema Kohli has created her own niche in the world of contemporary art since past 35 years. Her creative repertoire is eclectic, encompassing a wide range of mediums ranging from painting, murals, experiential installation performances, films to installations, sculptures and each a unique expression of her style. She has over time brought a synergy of her unique sensibilities in her art. There is a celebration of beauty, in her art. Working with oils on canvas, inks, mixed mediums, ceramics and printmaking, her work has redefined the basic contours of figurative art in India, finding admirers across the planet.

Seema Kohli has had over 30 solo shows in Venice, Brussels, London, New York, Dubai, Singapore, Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad and many more. She has participated in International Biennials (Venice, Shanghai, India), Art fairs (Hong Kong, Basel, Beijing, Madrid, India). Her work can be seen as murals of 10' x100' at the T3 Delhi International Airport, Mumbai International/Domestic Airport. Her works are in collections of the National Gallery Of Modern Art, Bangalore, Melinda Bill Gates Foundation, Rubin's Museum, MOSA-Brussels and Kochi Museum of Arts. She lives and works in New Delhi.
